प्रधानमंत्री उच्च शिक्षा प्रोत्साहन योजना 2026

Under this national scholarship initiative, the Government of India provides monetary assistance of ₹2,500/- monthly / annually directly into the bank accounts of deserving students to facilitate higher education and alleviate financial burdens.

यह योजना क्यों? (Why This Scheme?)

  • छात्रों की शिक्षा को मजबूत बनाने के लिए

    To strengthen and stabilize the education standards of young minds by easing monetary pressures.

  • आर्थिक सहायता से सपनों को पंख देने के लिए

    To give wings to the career dreams of financial needy students with constant educational aid.

  • शिक्षा के प्रति प्रोत्साहन और उज्जवल भविष्य के लिए

    To incentivize standard education and secure a brighter future for the youth of India.

कैसे प्राप्त करें? (How to Apply?)

  • 1

    अपनी जानकारी सही तरीके से भरें

    Fill in your personal, academic, and bank account details accurately on the portal form below.

  • 2

    अपना बैंक खाता सक्रिय रखें

    Keep your submitted bank account active and linked with your Aadhaar Card number for DBT verification.

  • 3

    पात्रता पूरी होने पर राशि सीधे आपके खाते में आएगी

    Upon verification of eligibility criteria, the scholarship amount (₹2500/-) will be credited directly to your bank account.

Self-Declaration / स्व-घोषणा

1. Declaration of Eligibility: I hereby declare that I am a citizen of India and currently enrolled as a regular student in the educational institution specified in the application. I affirm that the annual family income does not exceed the limit specified under the Prime Minister Scholarship Scheme guidelines.

2. Accuracy of Information: I verify that all inputs (Aadhaar number, bank account details, marks details) provided in this form are accurate and complete to the best of my knowledge. I understand that supplying false information will lead to immediate cancellation of my scholarship, recovery of credited amounts, and potential legal actions under appropriate Government statutes.

3. Aadhaar Consent: I give my consent to the Ministry of Education, Government of India, to use my Aadhaar Number for demographic authentication and linking with the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) for DBT scholarship transfers.
